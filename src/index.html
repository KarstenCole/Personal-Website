CYBERSTART

Cyberstart is a yearly opened program dedicated to teaching students about cybersecurity. It allows schools across the US to take part, and awards the students which do exceptionally well in the cybersecurity challenges.


As a participant in Cyberstart I was awarded the Silver Award, and the National Cyber Scholarship. The Silver Award is awarded to students who reach 50,000 points or up. The National Cyber Scholarship is awarded to students in the top 7%, and gives students $3,000 worth of cybersecurity training.
